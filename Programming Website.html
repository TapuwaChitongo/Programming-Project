Some words about ME

My name is Tapuwa Chitongo, a 20-year old Entreprenuer currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science. As a self-described social butterfly, I find it easy to interact with people and speak to crowds confidently.
I am fairly good at coordinating different ideas into an event that captures the mood, message and individualistic style of my natural creative nature.
With a flexible, adaptable get it done attitude, I welcome any challenges that may inevitably arise. I am not one to shy from a problem, I seek out creative solutions that many others overlook and deem as impossible to overcome.
Through leadership experience I have attained skills in servant leadership as well as the ability to facilitate and work with a team.
